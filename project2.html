Challenges Faced & Problem-Solving Approach

Challenges

I was new to Tableau, so I found it hard to get used to its layout and how things work. Since I had worked with Power BI before, Tableau felt quite different.

Approach

To improve my skills in Tableau, I compared its features with those in Power BI, which I was already familiar with. This helped me understand how to use Tableau more effectively. I also watched a YouTube tutorial to deepen my knowledge. This process showed my ability to learn new tools quickly. After gaining more confidence, I chose the best charts and graphs for my data. I first planned out the dashboard design using Figma, allowing me to organize my ideas visually before building the actual dashboard in Tableau.
